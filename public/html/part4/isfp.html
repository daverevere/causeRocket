"Well, Gron," you begin. "I like to chew and swallow my bites. It tastes like candy!"

Gron gasps and Garge grabs your shoulders. "You swallowed asteroid?" he cries.

"Only a little," you reply sheepishly.

"Asteroid can't be eaten!" cries Grong.

You feel your stomach rumble and rise just then. Putting a hand on Garge's shoulder, you throw up a pile of sticky, rock mush. At the same time, a deep, unhappy song pours at your mouth.

"I see what you mean," you cough.

"The asteriods are alive," Gron explains. "The crystals are the songs they make. We crunch to hear their music, not to eat their flesh."

"That's not what I meant, {{user.name}}," says Garge. "The better way to mine is... together!"

"Oh," you reply. "Right!"

"There is only one miner to asteriod," Garge continues. "I liked two, better."

"Three is nice too," Gron adds.

You mine with Garge and Gron for a bit, but soon you feel the time has come to return home. Garge and Gron set your rocket upright and hug you goodbye.

"Farewell, asteriod eater!" Gron calls as you climb inside the rocket.

"We'll miss you friend!" cries Garge.

You wave and close the hatch. Looking at the single button lit up on the panel, you consider all that you've learned about yourself.
